What We Do

The Chess Club is dedicated to helping students develop strategic thinking, problem-solving skills, and mental discipline through the game of chess.

Activities Include:

  • Weekly chess practice sessions
  • Tournament preparation and competitions
  • Strategy workshops and lessons
  • Analysis of famous games
  • Friendly matches and challenges

Who Can Join?

All skill levels welcome! Whether you're a beginner learning the basics or an experienced player, there's a place for you in our club.
