What We Do

The Qur'an Club provides a welcoming space for students to deepen their connection with the Qur'an through recitation, memorization, and understanding its teachings.

Activities Include:

  • Qur'an recitation practice with proper Tajweed
  • Memorization support and Hifz programs

Who Can Join?

All Muslim students are welcome regardless of their current level of Qur'anic knowledge. Whether you're just starting to learn or already proficient, join us in this blessed journey.
