What We Do

The Cubing Club is for students who love the challenge of speedcubing and puzzle-solving. Learn algorithms, improve your times, and connect with fellow cubers.

Activities Include:

  • Speedcubing practice sessions
  • Algorithm learning and tutorials
  • Solving different types of cubes (2x2, 3x3, 4x4, Pyraminx, etc.)
  • Timed competitions and challenges
  • Tips and techniques from advanced cubers
  • Record tracking and personal bests

Who Can Join?

Anyone interested in cubing! Whether you can solve a cube in 10 seconds or 10 minutes (or can't solve it at all yet), you're welcome to join and improve together.
