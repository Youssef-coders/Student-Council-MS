What We Do

The 3D Printing Club is a 10-week course where you'll learn 3D Modeling, Slicing, and 3D Printing. Combine creativity with technology and bring your ideas to life!

What You'll Learn:

  • 3D Modeling - Design your own models using professional software
  • Slicing - Prepare your designs for 3D printing
  • 3D Printing - Print your creations and take them home!
  • Design projects and creative challenges
  • Learn about materials and printing techniques
  • Receive calipers and tools to keep!

Course Details

  • 📚 Duration: 10 weeks
  • 👥 Open to: Grades 7, 8, and 9
  • 🕒 When: Once a week during Period 3
  • 📍 Where: STEM Room
  • 💻 Requirements: Computer/Laptop and Mouse

Who Can Join?

Students in grades 7, 8, and 9 who are curious about 3D printing and design! No prior experience required - we'll teach you everything from scratch.

💰 Entry Fee

100 SAR - This is a flat fee that covers all materials. You'll receive calipers and tools to keep!
