What We Do

The Debate Club helps students develop critical thinking, public speaking, and argumentation skills through structured debates and discussions on current events and important topics.

Activities Include:

  • Weekly debate practice sessions
  • Research and argument preparation
  • Public speaking workshops
  • Mock debates and competitions
  • Discussion of current events and social issues
  • Participation in interschool debate tournaments

Who Can Join?

All students interested in improving their communication skills! Whether you're comfortable speaking in front of crowds or want to overcome stage fright, we welcome you.
